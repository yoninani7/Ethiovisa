About Ethio-Visa Consultancy

Ethio-Visa Consultancy was established in 2010 with a mission to empower Ethiopian students to achieve their dreams of studying abroad. Over the years, we have helped thousands of students secure admissions to prestigious universities around the world.

Our team consists of experienced education consultants, visa experts, and former international students who understand the challenges and opportunities of studying abroad. We maintain strong relationships with educational institutions and immigration authorities worldwide.

  • 13+ years of experience in education consulting
  • Expert team with firsthand international education experience
  • Personalized approach for each student
  • Strong network of university partners worldwide
  • Comprehensive support from inquiry to enrollment
  • Proven track record of visa success
